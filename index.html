Professional Experience

Researcher

As an undergraduate researcher in the Antibiotic Discovery stream at the University of Utah, I contribute to the search for novel antimicrobial compounds by isolating and characterizing soil-derived bacteria using culture-dependent methods. This research involves selective enrichment techniques, high-throughput screening against ESKAPE pathogens, and bioassay-guided fractionation to identify active compounds. Through this experience, I’ve developed hands-on proficiency in microbiology and molecular biology techniques, while contributing to clinically relevant research aimed at combating antibiotic resistance.

Senator

As a Senator in the Associated Students of the University of Utah (ASUU), I represent the College of Fine Arts in the university’s student government. In this role, I advocate for student interests, participate in legislative processes, and help oversee the allocation of college-level funding through the College Student Council. My work includes legislative drafting, event planning, student advocacy, and serving on committees that shape university-wide policy and governance.

Science Ambassador

As a Science Ambassador for the University of Utah’s College of Science, I serve as a representative for prospective and current students, supporting recruitment, outreach, and student engagement efforts. I lead campus tours, mentor first-year and transfer students, and assist with New Student Orientation and themed community initiatives. I also participate in K–12 STEM outreach events and university-sponsored programs that promote science education and community involvement.
